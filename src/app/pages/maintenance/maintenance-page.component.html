Maintenance

The Maintenance Terminal serves as a centralized hub for managing and maintaining your Pip-Boy. Whether you're performing routine upkeep or troubleshooting issues, these tools are designed to keep the device running smoothly and provide access to its core features and functionality.

These tools are intended for advanced users, field engineers, and Vault-Tec-certified technicians. If you're new to Pip-Boy customization, it's recommended you consult the Operator's Manual or perform changes in a controlled test vault before deployment.
