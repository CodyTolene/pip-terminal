Quick Support

Have questions about your Pip-Boy 3000 Mk V? Browse the topics below for quick answers to common issues like resets, music conversion, unresponsive behavior, and where to get additional support.

How do I reset my device?

Use The Wand Company's websites official Pip-Boy tool. Visit the URL below, connect your device, and select "Upgrade":
https://thewandcompany.com/pip-boy/upgrade/?erase

How do I convert music for my Pip-Boy?

Install FFmpeg:
https://ffmpeg.org/download.html

Covnert the file to wav format:
ffmpeg -i "input.mp3" -ac 1 -ar 16000 -sample_fmt s16 -c:a pcm_s16le -f wav output.wav

You can modify the volume using the -af "volume=10dB" param. For example to increase the volume:
ffmpeg -i "input.mp3" -ac 1 -ar 16000 -af "volume=10dB" -sample_fmt s16 -c:a pcm_s16le -f wav output.wav

My Pip-Boy is unresponsive, what do I do?

Things to try

  • Hold the power button down for 30-60 seconds, until it restarts.
  • If button restart fails, unplug the battery and plug it back in. The battery can be easily accessed by removing one screw on the back of the device.
  • Try connecting via USB and manually reinstalling the Pip OS: https://archive.org/details/pip-os-v-1.0.
