Pip-Boy 3000 Companion

Community Companion Project

In development, updates rolling out as we learn more.

This is a planned community-built terminal aimed at helping you manage apps, files, and mods for compatible Pip-Boy devices. Support for the upcoming Pip-Boy 3000 release depends on device firmware capabilities and what The Wand Company enables at launch. If an official companion app ships, or if access is restricted, some or all features here may be delayed or unavailable on release.
