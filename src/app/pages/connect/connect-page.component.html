Connect

The Connect Terminal allows you to establish a direct connection with your Pip-Boy 3000 Mk V device.

When connected, you can install apps, transfer files, play media, run diagnostics, and access deeper functionality for a customized Pip-Boy experience.

To begin, ensure your device is powered on and connected via USB, then tap the button below to initiate the connection sequence.
