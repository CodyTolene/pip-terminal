We use Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors interact with our site. Google Analytics collects the following types of information:

  • Device and browser info (e.g., OS, screen resolution, browser type)

  • Pages visited and interaction times
  • Referring sites

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, emails, or phone numbers through analytics.

2. How We Use This Information

We use the analytics data to:

This helps us provide a better experience for all users.

3. Use of Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies in your browser to store anonymous data. These cookies help us:

  • Identify repeat visits
  • Track session duration
  • Understand user flow across pages

You may opt out of cookies by adjusting your browser settings or using a browser extension such as Google Analytics Opt-out .

4. Data Sharing and Storage

Google Analytics data is processed and stored by Google LLC in accordance with their own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

We do not sell or share your data with third-party advertisers.

5. Legal Compliance

We make every effort to comply with:

If you would like to request data removal or access, please contact us.
